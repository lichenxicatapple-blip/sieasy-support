# SIEasy Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

## Introduction

SIEasy ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

## Local Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:
- Study progress and history
- Flashcard learning status
- Favorite questions
- Quiz and mock exam scores
- App settings and preferences

We do not have access to your personal study records stored locally.

## iCloud Sync

SIEasy offers optional iCloud synchronization to keep your learning data in sync across your Apple devices.

### What data is synced via iCloud:
- Study progress and quiz scores
- Flashcard data and learning history
- Favorite questions
- App settings and preferences

### Important notes:
- iCloud sync is **optional** and can be disabled in the app's Settings
- When disabled, all data remains stored locally on your device only
- Your synced data is stored in your personal iCloud account, managed by Apple
- We do not have access to your iCloud data
- For information about how Apple handles iCloud data, please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

### Data deletion:
- You can delete all synced data by disabling iCloud sync and clearing data in the app's Settings
- Alternatively, you can delete the app's iCloud data through iOS Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage

## AI Assistant Feature

When you use the AI Assistant feature, your questions are sent to third-party AI service providers. These providers may include:
- OpenAI
- Google
- Anthropic
- DeepSeek

We do not store your AI conversations on our servers. Please review the privacy policies of these AI service providers for information about how they handle your data.

## Analytics and Tracking

We do not collect any analytics or tracking data. We do not use any third-party analytics services.

## Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party AI services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
