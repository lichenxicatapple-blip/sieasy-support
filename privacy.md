# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 2025**

## Overview

SIEasy ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally
- Study progress and history
- Flashcard learning status
- Favorite questions
- App settings and preferences

This data is stored **only on your device** and is not transmitted to our servers.

### AI Assistant
When you use the AI Assistant feature, your questions are sent to third-party AI service providers (such as OpenAI, Google, or Anthropic) to generate responses. We do not store these
conversations on our servers.

### Analytics
We do not collect any analytics or tracking data.

## Data Security

All your study data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal study records.

## Third-Party Services

The AI Assistant feature uses third-party APIs. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:
- [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
- [DeepSeek Privacy Policy](https://www.deepseek.com/privacy)

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
