## FAQ

**Q: What is SIEasy?**
A: SIEasy is a smart study app designed for the FINRA SIE (Securities Industry Essentials) exam.

**Q: How do I use the AI assistant?**
A: While answering questions, tap the "AI Assistant" button to get instant explanations and help.

**Q: Can I import my own study materials?**
A: Yes, you can import PDF textbooks in the "Materials" tab.

**Q: How do I reset my study progress?**
A: Go to Settings > Clear Study Data.
